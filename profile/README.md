# Bit Thicket

**Building bold technology that stewards what matters.**

We are Bit Thicket, LLC — a lean, determined technology company turning ambitious ideas into real, useful products. From intelligent personal finance tools to AI-powered consulting and custom software solutions, we create software that empowers people and businesses to take control of their future.

### Our Flagship Product
**Steward** — Your intelligent personal finance companion.  
Steward connects your financial world, surfaces actionable insights, and helps you make smarter decisions with clarity and confidence. Built with modern tools (including Plaid integration), it’s designed for individuals who want to move beyond basic budgeting into true financial stewardship.

### What We Do

- **AI Consulting & Solutions** — Expert guidance and implementation at [bitthicket.ai](https://bitthicket.ai)
- Custom software, automation, and intelligent systems for forward-thinking clients
